Microsoft Outlook patch KB3203467 has a known bug, and can make it so that you cannot open attachments in invitations. Uninstall it to fix. Here is how to do this on Windows 7 with Outlook 2010:

  1. Click Start
  2. Choose Control Panel from the right portion of the menu
  3. Click on ‘Category’ in the upper right and change it to ‘Small Icons’
  4. Click Windows Update
  5. Click ‘View Update History’ from the left menu
  6. Click the ‘Installed Updates’ hyperlink over the list of Installed Updates
  7. Find the update (KB3203467) Under the Microsoft Office Professional section
  8. Highlight that update and click ‘Uninstall’ from the toolbar over the list of updates.
