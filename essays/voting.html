The Electronic Voting Crisis

An excellent way to take over a democracy is take control of its voting system. Stuffing ballot boxes isn't new, but now we have a high-tech way to control every ballot box in a country: electronic voting machines. Stuffing physical ballot boxes requires a lot of dangerous work and is hard to to do undetected; changing an electronic value to a "desirable" value can be done by one person. And given some of today's elecronic voting sytsems, it's impossible to detect.
