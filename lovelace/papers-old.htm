Publications by David A. Wheeler

The following is a list of my publications. This list does not include my many papers and reports which have not been released due to their classified, sensitive, informal, or specialized nature.

Books

  • Co-Author, The GNOME User's Guide.
  • Author, Ada 95: The Lovelace Tutorial, 1997, Springer-Verlag (New York), ISBN 0-387-94801-5. More information about how to order this book is available.
  • Co-author and co-editor, Software Inspection: An Industry Best Practice, May 1996, IEEE Computer Society Press (Los Alamitos, CA), ISBN 0-8186-7340-0, IEEE Catalog Number BP07340, Library of Congress Number 95-41054. You can order this book from the IEEE by emailing to cs.books@computer.org, calling U.S. (714) 821-8380, or faxing to U.S. (714) 821-4641.
