Sigma Software Package Disk - 1983

This disk contains the Apple II software sold by my high school science club. We wrote all of these programs ourselves (ok, Manson wrote most of them, I wrote a few) There are some classic games here, as well as stirling examples of what can happen when silly high school students get their hands on a computer.

Minimum requirements: Apple II+ with 48k RAM **

(** archive is a DOS 3.3 disk but must be expanded on a ProDOS system using ShrinkIt)

Download: SIGMA.SDK - ShrinkIt archive, or SIGMA.ZIP - Zipped disk image for emulators, ProDOS order

Just a few of the exciting and novel programs available to you at no charge!

  • ALIEN DEATH - You're gonna die. Just pick where.. but choose wisely!
  • QUELPNEG DEFENSE - Battle fierce aliens before they can destroy the Earth!
  • SAVAGE TERROR - Must be seen to be believed!
  • PLUTONIAN RAIDERS - Choose wisely and defend your moonbase
  • CIRCUS APPLE - Collect that garbage!
  • BILL THE MONK - Watch Bill the Monk on his way to the monestary [sic]
  • NOOB ESSAY - >Random sentences with sometimes hilarious results!
  • DISK DUSTER 4.3 - A serious utility. A must for all Apple II users. Will work with ProDOS disks.
