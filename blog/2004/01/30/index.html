New feature: A blog!

Those of you who have been following my website will notice a brand-new feature… a blog! I intend to use this blog to help people keep track of “new things” that happen on the website, as well as let people hear my latest ramblings (and find them later). Each entry will have a static location, so you can link to it for later reference.

I’m not supporting community discussions. I don’t have the disk space, it’s not clear I have the bandwidth, and most importantly, that is much more complicated to secure.
